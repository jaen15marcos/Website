Function: QuotedSpreads¶

Description¶

The QuotedSpreads function processes a table of market quotes to calculate the dollar quoted spread (dqs) and the midpoint (mt) between the national best bid (nat_best_bid) and the national best offer (nat_best_offer) or bid (bid_price) and ask_price (ask_price). It then calculates the weighted average of these spreads over time.

Parameters¶

  • input.table: A table containing market quote data with the following columns:
    • nat_best_bid : National best bid price.
    • nat_best_offer: National best offer price.
    • time: Timestamp of the quote.
    • sym: Symbol of the security.
  • input.columns: A list of minimum length 2 and maximum length 3 containing the following column names in the predefined order:
    • nat_best_bid : National best bid price.
    • nat_best_offer: National best offer price.
    • mkt(Optional): Venue where trade took place, only add if granularity is desired.
  • input.start.time: Start time to be considered (i.e., quotes before this time are not taken into account).
  • input.end.time: End time to be considered (i.e., quotes after this time are not taken into account).

Returns¶

A table with the following columns, grouped by sym and date:

  • sym: Symbol of the security.
  • date: Date of the quote.
  • dqs: Dollar quoted spread for the given time period.
  • pqs: Percentage quoted spread for the given time period.

Syntax¶

input.table: input.table; input.columns: bid_priceask_price or bid_priceask_price`mkt; input.start.time: 09:30:00.000; input.end.time: 16:00:00.000; result = QuotedSpreads[input.table;input.columns;input.start.time;input.end.time)];

Function: EffectiveSpreads¶

Description¶

The EffectiveSpread function processes a table of trade and quote data to calculate the effective spreads for each trade. It updates the table with the midpoint price, determines the tick test (whether the trade is a buy or sell), computes the effective spread in both dollar and percentage terms, and then aggregates these effective spreads using a volume-weighted average.

Parameters¶

  • input.table: A table containing trade and quote data with the following columns:
    • nat_best_bid: National best bid price.
    • nat_best_offer: National best offer price.
    • price: Trade price.
    • volume: Trade volume.
    • sym: Symbol of the security.
    • time: Timestamp of the trade.
    • listing_mkt: Listing market of the security.

Returns¶

A table with the following columns, grouped by sym, date, and listing_mkt:

  • sym: Symbol of the security.
  • date: Date of the trade.
  • listing_mkt: Listing market of the security.
  • des_k: Volume-weighted average of the dollar effective spread.
  • pes_k: Volume-weighted average of the percentage effective spread.

Syntax¶

input.table: input.table; input.columns: bid_priceask_price or bid_priceask_price`mkt; input.start.time: 09:30:00.000; input.end.time: 16:00:00.000; result = EffectiveSpread[input.table;input.columns;input.start.time;input.end.time)];

Notes¶

  • The tick_test calculation uses conditional logic to determine whether each trade is a buy or sell based on the comparison of trade prices to the midpoint.
  • Effective spreads are calculated and then aggregated using volume-weighted averages to provide a meaningful summary for each symbol, date, and listing market.
