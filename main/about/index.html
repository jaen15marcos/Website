Mine was BAR which, perhaps, now has too many memories of the champagne lifestyle in investment banking in the 1990s. At Capco my acronym was GBKR, so that became the domain name by default.

I have been working with software since 1976 and working in electronic execution since 2002. You can find a short résumé here. And, yes, I still code.
